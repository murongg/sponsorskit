# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Open source users, prospective sponsors, and visitors who want to see and visit the people or organizations supporting the maintainer's work.

## Product Purpose

Present sponsor recognition in one focused public page and one embeddable README image. Success means every sponsor is easy to identify, their profile is easy to visit, and the page stays simple to maintain from one configuration file.

## Brand Personality

Simple, neutral, and restrained. The experience should communicate gratitude with quiet confidence and keep attention on the sponsors.

## Anti-references

Avoid ornamental card grids, decorative gradients, noisy backgrounds, and exaggerated motion that competes with sponsor identities.

## Design Principles

- Keep sponsors as the visual focus.
- Preserve one consistent presentation across the website and generated SVG.
- Prefer direct, lightweight interactions over decorative UI.
- Make recognition and outbound navigation immediately understandable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain keyboard-visible focus, meaningful alternative text, sufficient contrast, and reduced-motion-friendly behavior.
